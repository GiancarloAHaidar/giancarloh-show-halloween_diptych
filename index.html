Halloween Diptych

Giancarlo A. Haidar

Show Tell
X
  • The Halloween Diptych Poster is a minimalist, suspenseful design showcasing iconic slashers and their weapons in pursuit of an injured protagonist. It draws inspiration from simplistic classic movie posters and the immersive interaction of horror video games. The poster tells two stories of survival or fear, and combining the two posters creates a chilling scene of relentless pursuit, tension, and dread.
